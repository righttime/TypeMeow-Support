Pomodoro, but gentle

Breaks, the quiet way.

After 25 minutes of focus, your companion curls up and a tiny heart appears in the menu bar. No alarms, no banners, nothing in your notification center. Just a friend on break.

  • No pop-ups, ever. The menu bar heart is the whole notification.
  • The 3-minute rule. Coffee refills don't reset your session — walking away does.
  • 25 → 5 · 50 → 10. Longer focus blocks earn longer rests, automatically.
