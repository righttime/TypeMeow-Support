## What is TypeMeow?

TypeMeow is a tiny animal companion that lives in your Mac's menu bar. It watches you type — not *what* you type, just *how fast* — and reacts:

- 🐱 **Cat**, 🐸 **Frog**, or 🐕 **Siba** — pick your companion
- Your animal moves its paws as you type, faster when you're on fire
- **KPM** (Keystrokes Per Minute) displayed right in the menu bar
- **Pomodoro breaks** — after 25 minutes of focused work, your companion quietly rests and shows a little heart ❤️
- **Big preview** — an optional floating window with your animal, draggable and resizable

No pop-ups. No badges. No annoying notifications. Just a quiet friend working alongside you.

## Privacy Policy

TypeMeow is built with a **privacy-first** philosophy:

| What | TypeMeow |
|------|----------|
| Keystroke content | ❌ **Never seen, never stored** — only key *counts* are measured |
| Network calls | ❌ **Zero** — no servers, no cloud, no analytics, no tracking |
| Data storage | 📁 **100% local** — stats stored in `~/Library/Application Support/TypeMeow/` on your Mac only |
| Active app tracking | ❌ **Never** — we don't know which app you're typing in |
| Third-party SDKs | ❌ **None** — built with Apple SDK only |

**In short: Your typing is yours. Period.**

For the full privacy policy, see [PRIVACY.md](PRIVACY.md).
